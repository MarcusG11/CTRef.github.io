About CSRP

The Connecticut State Soccer Association and Connecticut Junior Soccer Association jointly established a State Referee Program; the "CSRP" web went live on November 24, 2003

Purpose of the Connecticut State Referee Program

- To carry out the National Referee Development Program of the United States Soccer Federation through the State Referee Program

-To develop the quality and quantity of Federation Referees, Assessors, Instructors, and Assignors; to register those Referees, Assessors, Instructors, and Assignors required to serve the two State Associations and to achieve excellence in Officiating, Governance, Education, and Administration
